Introduction
============

CV Heap is a brand new approach to finding your perfect job. We use every single
detail about your entire job history to match you with the job opportunities
where you are the best fit.

.. admonition:: Sign-Up Form

   CV Heap is under active development--we are taking the time to "do this right".

   `Sign up to be notified when CV Heap is released! <https://forms.gle/MjA6wfkfR5Hrjcny5>`__

How Is CV Heap Different?
-------------------------

No Social Media
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

   Where most other "job sites" are social media platforms that benefit from
   keeping users unemployed and guessing how to game the system, CV Heap only
   exists to provide top-quality matches.

Full Career History
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

   You are more than a terse summary of job tasks. It takes a complete
   understanding of your entire history to accurately identify the job where you
   will be a perfect candidate.

   We care about every technology you every touched, every complex problem you
   ever solved, every mudane daily activity, how you felt about meetings, every
   teaching / learning opportunity, how you prefer to work, etc.

Full Job Description
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

   Most job posts try to advertise why someone would want to work at their
   company. Job posts on CV Heap are limited to thorough descriptions of what
   day-to-day life is like.

See Mutual Interest
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

   When both job seeker and employer "Show Interest" in one another, **both**
   are able to see this mutual interest. This helpse prevent one-sided leverage
   during the hiring process.

Foster Friendly Departures
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

   Anyone who provides a thorough summary of the job they are vacating will be
   rewarded with credit for a free month of premium, to be used within 10 years.

Honest Database
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

   - **NO** Promoted Jobs
   - **NO** Ghost Jobs
   - **NO** Hidden Jobs
 
     There will **NEVER** be a paywall to see your best matches. We return only
     the best matches and leave further refinement to you.

   - Matching is reduced down to a single "score card"

     A very large score card sits at the heart of our operation. This allows us
     to match your CV data against every job in our database using thousands of
     data points.

Privacy by Design
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

   We will **NEVER** sell your data. **If** data is ever made public, it will be
   in a blog post on cvheap.net and strictly limited to aggregate score card
   data--in hopes that all users will find value in this data.

Frequently Asked Questions
--------------------------

Are Résumés Eliminated?
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

   **No**, not at all! We use your entire history to perform in-depth matching.
   Hiring Managers still need to know why **You** are the best fit for **Their** role.

Why Git?
~~~~~~~~

   Using external Git services allows us to keep the data in your control, and it
   helps ensure your CV data cannot be leaked--a non-web process fetches the
   update and generates a numbers-only score card.

   We currently support GitHub/GitLab because these are the "industry standard"
   git platforms, and they provide everything we need within free tier.

Get Started Today
-----------------

CV Heap is one piece of a larger puzzle. Our goal is to `do one thing well
<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Unix_philosophy>`__.

**This is every step you need to find the perfect job FOR YOU:**

1. Create Content
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Build a complete summary of your entire professional career.

    - The best LLM-Assisted tool currently on the market for building Résumés
      is `Hirecarta <https://hirecarta.com/>`__.
    - Write your **COMPLETE** story! (Example: `My Personal CV
      <https://cv.lustfield.net/>`__)
    - We recommend saving this into a **PRIVATE** [`GitHub`_ or `GitLab`_]
      named "**cvheap**".

2. Find Open Positions
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Sign up to be notified when CV Heap is released.

    - Sign-up form: `CV Heap - Release Notification <https://forms.gle/MjA6wfkfR5Hrjcny5>`__
    - We use Google Forms to prevent spam and to ensure this data is easily deleted.

3. Build Your Résumé
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Make your résumé clearly show what makes you the perfect match.

    - We recommend `Hudson Valley Career Development <hudsonvalleycd.com>`__
      for a fresh take on what it takes to stand out.
    - Check out `My Personal (generic) Résumé
      <https://resume.lustfield.net/>`__ for an example.
